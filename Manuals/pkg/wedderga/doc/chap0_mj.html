Abstract

``Wedderga'' stands for ``WEDDERburn decomposition of Group Algebras". This is a GAP package to compute the simple components of the Wedderburn decomposition of semisimple group algebras of finite groups over finite fields and over subfields of finite cyclotomic extensions of the rationals. It also contains functions that produce the primitive central idempotents of semisimple group algebras and a complete set of orthogonal primitive idempotents. Other functions of Wedderga allow to construct crossed products over a group with coefficients in an associative ring with identity and the multiplication determined by a given action and twisting.
