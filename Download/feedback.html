--- title: Installation Feedback layout: default toc: Installation ---

We ask you to inform us when you download and install GAP. Please write a brief email letter to the address support@gap-system.org informing us about

  • your name, e-mail address and institution,
  • the type of installation (used operating system and computer(s)),
  • the number of people who will use this installation,
  • earlier versions of GAP installed by you, if any,
  • your area of (mathematical) interest that led you to the installation of GAP,
  • possibly remarks and suggestions concerning the installation of GAP.
