Feedback

The GAP Group is interested in all your comments and suggestions. In particular we ask you to consider the following types of feedback.

  • If you publish a mathematical result that was partly obtained using GAP, please cite GAP, just as you would cite a paper that you used (see recommended citation for GAP).

  • Please, tell us about publications in which GAP was cited, as described on the page feedback by writing to support@gap-system.org.
