Contents

This library is part of the core GAP system. It contains almost all perfect groups of size up to 10^6.

Authors of the data listings

Derek F. Holt and Wilhelm Plesken.

Author of the GAP implementation

Volkmar Felsch.

Source

Derek F. Holt and W. Plesken: Perfect Groups, Oxford Math. Monographs, Oxford University Press, 1989.
