Contents

This library is part of the core GAP system. It contains Q-class representatives of all irreducible maximal finite integral matrix groups of dimension up to 31 and Z-class representatives of those among these groups which are of dimension at most 11 or of dimension 13, 17, 19, or 23.

Authors of the data listings

Wilhelm Plesken, Bernd Souvignier, and Gabriele Nebe.

Author of the GAP implementation

Volkmar Felsch.
